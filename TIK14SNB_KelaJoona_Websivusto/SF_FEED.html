Ambiance Released, new tour dates revealed!

Ahoy! Yesterday we achieved a great milestone by releasing our first official album, called Ambiance (read "4MßI4NCE"), and are happy to tell you that we'll start touring on next year!
A big hand for everyone who has or will support us by buying the new release. We are longing to see as many of you attending our gigs as possible!
Anyway, here's the dates, you've earned them! ;)
  7/1/2015 - Martha's Grill and Cafe 4 PM
  8/1/2015 - Harold Simmons' home party, at his house Lodgington Road 8221A7 8 PM
  10/1/2015 - The Grand opening of Brasburg Thinner Factory 2 PM
  14/1/2015 - The Central Park, unless it's raining 10 AM

New single release planned, out in Feb 2015

The yet untitled single will feature 3 original Sarcastic Front tracks and 1 cover track. According to our drummer, Lad, it will have more complex songs than our previous releases.

"You know, we'd jam together an' then Jazsty comes up with new idea, like he always does. Though this time, it'll be more progressive and longer sh*t we've played."
"New direction, you say? Naww, I dunno anything 'bout taking new tracks, I mean, we couldn't abandon our influences. It's more like that we'll try out new things and then decide if it sounds good or not."

He also sends his regards to our fans who appeared at our first gig ever at Han's Meathouse on 1st of July, despite the heavy smell of rotting cows.

"Not only the band was heavy, but the air aswell! Ehehaha!"
